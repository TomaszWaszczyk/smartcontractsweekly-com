    Background

    With years of experience building and marketing products as part of in-house teams, we founded Narative both to pursue our own product ambitions, and help promising companies to pursue theirs.

    What does third wave mean for technology?

    A company’s culture isn’t something we think should be passed down as commandments, or enforced like law. It’s the choices we make every day that define who we are — as individuals, and as a tea

    Be kind

    Communicate honestly but sensitively; act with positive intent; and trust others to do the same.

    Be creative

    Push beyond best practices and by-the-numbers design to discover valuable new ideas.

    Be adaptable

    Strive to grow from every challenge and change, even when it means changing your mind.

    Be yourself

    Work and live the way that uniquely suits you, free of rigid hierarchies and arbitrary expectations.
